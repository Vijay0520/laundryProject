Few facts about the world we live in

Earth, the third planet from the Sun, is a unique celestial body known for supporting life. It's a rocky planet with a diverse atmosphere and a large amount of liquid water, covering about 71% of its surface. The Earth's unique conditions, including its distance from the Sun and its atmosphere, allow for the existence of liquid water and a biosphere of diverse plant and animal life.
